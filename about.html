About Us

Crafting Homes That redefines lifestyle

Gangothri is a fast growing conglomerate with a multi vertical presence in Real Estate, Housing, Nutrients & Fertilizers and Aqua Culture since 1992, with considerable Y-O-Y growth in alignment with our business strategy. With our presence for the last 29 years in business, we are adapting to the changing customer expectations while achieving the highest customer satisfaction with quality deliverables on time. Gangothri Developers successfully completed 900 HMDA approved open plot ventures and 600 gated community apartments with satisfactory results. With the experience gained over a period of 16 years in real estate and thorough understanding of the increasing customer expectations, we are moving to build high rise luxury communities in Hyderabad’s growth corridors to offer pleasure to live-in gated communities with plethora of amenities to deliver ‘Homes with Lifestyle’. Gangothri, is led by a team of highly qualified professionals with proven track record. Our leadership has held several leadership positions at highly reputed Multinational Corporations. Every member of Gangothri’s team is a valued asset, who is encouraged to accomplish the business goals while adhering to the core values of the company.
